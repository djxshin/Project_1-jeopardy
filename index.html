Welcome to Quentin Tarantino film themed Jeopardy!!

First select whether you'll be playing as Single player or Dual Player mode.

Once you chose your player mode...you'll be able to select from different categories of film directed by Quentin Tarantino.

* For each category (films) it'll have four different point system, points will differ as well as the difficulty by each of the rows which will start from 100 pts and the points will go up on consecutive order as follow:

* 100pts
* 200ts
* 300pts
* 400pts

The game ends when all the questions are answered, and the points are tallied up at the end.
